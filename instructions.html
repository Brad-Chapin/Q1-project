How d'ye play this game, anyway?

Aright, matey, pay attention- I dinna like repeatin' meself. Th' goal 'ere is t' collect the booty by scorin' the most points over five turns. Each turn consists o' three throws o' th' bones. Ye need a Ship (a six), a Cap'n (a five), and a Crew (a four). Ye must collect 'em in that order, though ye CAN get more'n one in a single toss. Once ye have yer Ship, Cap'n, 'n' Crew, th remainin' bones, added t'gether, are yer score. If ye have any tosses remainin', and ye dinna like the score yer showin', ye can choose to toss th' bones again, but be wary- if yer new toss is lower, yer stuck with it. Once ye either collect yer points, or fail to get any because ye couldn't get yer Ship t'gether, play passes t'yer opponent. Whiche'er scallywag has th' most points after five turns wins th' booty an' braggin' rights.

Take all ye can, an' give nothin' back! Good fortunes to ye.
